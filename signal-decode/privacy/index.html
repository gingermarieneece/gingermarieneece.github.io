Signal Decode

Privacy Policy

Signal Decode helps you decode the vibe in a message and get a grounded next move. This page explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Summary

  • Your saved decode history is stored locally on your device.
  • Message text is sent to our secure backend only to generate a decode response in real time.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics in the app.
  • We do not sell personal information or use app data for tracking across apps or websites.

Information Processed

When you use Signal Decode, you may paste a message or short situation into the app. That text, along with the selected context such as Dating, Ex, or Work, is sent to our backend so the app can generate a decode result.

Saved history items, favorites, and reopened reads are stored locally on your device to support the app experience.

AI Processing

Signal Decode uses an AI service through our backend to generate results such as the likely meaning, reply guidance, and rewrite suggestions. Message content is processed for the purpose of returning the requested decode result.

We do not use your pasted messages for advertising.

Subscriptions and Payments

If you purchase premium features, billing, renewals, and trial eligibility are handled by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Sharing

If you choose to share a result image, the app passes that content to the iOS share sheet or destination you select. Sharing only happens when you explicitly choose to do it.

Retention and Control

Your saved history stays on your device unless you delete the app or remove that content. You can choose what to paste, what to save, and what to share.
